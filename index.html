🔒

Data Not Collected

PeptidePilot has no servers, no analytics, no third-party SDKs, and no advertising. Everything you log lives only on your device. Backups are user-initiated and saved wherever you choose. We literally cannot see what you track.

⏱

Schedules & alerts

Per-peptide recurring schedules with local notifications. Log, snooze, or skip from the banner.

𝑓

Reconstitution math

Vial size + BAC water + dose → units to draw. With warnings for unsafe combinations.

🧪

Vial inventory

Doses remaining, expiry countdown, optional photo of each vial.

📍

Site rotation

10-site grid. The app suggests the next site based on your history.

📊

Insights & streaks

Daily-dose chart, 7/30-day adherence, current and best streak.

🌀

Side-effects journal

Quick 1–5 ratings for energy, mood, sleep, GI. See per-peptide correlations.

🌱

Cycle planner

"Weeks on / weeks off" — schedule auto-pauses during off weeks.

🏅

Quiet medals

Hex-shaped achievements for streaks, adherence, awareness, rotation.

⌚

Apple Watch + widgets

Log a dose from your wrist. Lock Screen widget & Dynamic Island countdown.

💾

Backup & export

JSON to iCloud Drive. PDF + CSV for your clinician.

🔐

Face ID lock

Optional biometric gate when launching or returning from background.

💻

Mac + iPad

Same app, runs on macOS via Mac Catalyst.
