Frequently asked

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything is stored on your device only. The app has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no third-party SDKs. Read the full privacy policy.

Will my data sync between my iPhone and Mac / Apple Watch?

Today's app is single-device by default. The Apple Watch companion mirrors iPhone data when paired. Mac Catalyst and iPhone share data through your Apple ID's app group container when both are signed into the same iCloud — but if your data isn't appearing across devices, use Settings → Backup → Create backup on the source device, save to iCloud Drive, and restore on the destination.

I lost my data after deleting the app

iOS deletes all app data when the app is removed. Without a backup, that data is gone — there's no cloud copy because we don't have a cloud. Going forward, use Settings → Backup → Create backup periodically to a location you control (iCloud Drive is recommended). Restore puts everything back exactly as it was.

Notifications aren't firing

Three things to check:

  • iPhone Settings → Notifications → PeptidePilot — make sure Allow Notifications is on.
  • In PeptidePilot, edit the peptide and confirm Notifications is enabled in the Schedule editor.
  • Make sure Focus / Do Not Disturb isn't suppressing them — check Focus settings on iPhone.

The reconstitution calculator gave a weird answer

Double-check that the units (mg / mcg / IU) match what's printed on your vial label. The calculator treats IU as 1:1 with mcg by default, which is correct for many peptides but not all — confirm your peptide's specific IU↔mass conversion before relying on the result.

How do I add a peptide that's not in the catalog?

In the Library tab, tap +. In the catalog picker, type the peptide's name in the "Custom peptide name" field at the top, then tap Use "<name>". You'll get a fully editable peptide entry with no preset catalog info.

Can I track non-peptide injectables (TRT, B12, etc.)?

Yes. Use the custom peptide flow — name it whatever you like, set the dose and schedule, and you're tracking. The app doesn't care what the substance is.

How do I cancel a schedule without deleting the history?

Open the peptide → tap the ••• menu → Pause. The peptide stays in your Library (greyed out under "Paused") with all its dose history intact, and notifications stop.

Why was Melanotan II / Retatrutide / [other peptide] removed from the catalog?

The bundled catalog covers a curated set of compounds with reasonably documented research. Some compounds are deliberately omitted because they're illegal to sell for human use in major markets, are pure investigational drugs not legally available, or don't have enough credible documentation for an educational summary. You can still track them as custom peptides — the app doesn't restrict that.

How do I export my data for my clinician?

Settings → Export → choose CSV (a spreadsheet of every dose log) or PDF (formatted report with peptide list and adherence). Both can be shared via email or AirDrop.

Is this medical advice?

No. PeptidePilot is a journal. It does not diagnose, treat, recommend, or replace a clinician. Consult a licensed clinician before starting, stopping, or changing any peptide protocol.
